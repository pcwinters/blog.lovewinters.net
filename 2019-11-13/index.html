Happy Birthday, Aurelia

By Patrick Winters On November 13, 2019

We're excitedly celebrating Aurelia's second birthday today, and we trust that the years to come will be kinder than what has passed. We turn the page on One, but we find an empty book and an uncertain future. Her unwritten story will be unique and full of joy and accomplishments, and I can't wait to read it. Auri's already rolling over, making silly faces, and feeding herself. She's the kind of child that proudly writes her own narrative and defies her limitations. We're thrilled for this terrific two!
